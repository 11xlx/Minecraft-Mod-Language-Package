该物品是一个[link{pneumaticcraft:menu/pressure_tubes}]管道模块[link{}]。也就是说，其只能被放置在压力管道上。点击链接查看关于管道模块的一般信息。

The Charging Module allows you to (dis)charge any item in any inventory it's pointing towards. By default it can (dis)charge up to 10mL/tick/item.
















[prefix{l}]Applying an    [link{item/advanced_pcb}]Advanced PCB[link{}][prefix{}]
When you apply an Advanced PCB to a Charging Module the maximum (dis)charging speed increases to 100mL/tick/item.
